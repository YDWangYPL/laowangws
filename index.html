What You Can Do

Start small. Everyone can be a guardian of the Earth.

Reduce Plastic Use

Bring your own bags and bottles. Avoid single-use plastics. Save hundreds of plastic bags from landfills each year.

Go Green on the Go

Use public transport, bike, or walk. Reduce carbon emissions. Cycling to work saves ~2kg CO₂ daily.

Save Energy

Use LED bulbs, turn off lights, choose energy-efficient appliances. Save 20% on bills and carbon footprint.

Sort Waste

Separate recyclables, hazardous waste, and food scraps. Boost recycling rates and reduce pollution.

Plant Trees

Join tree-planting events or adopt a tree. One mature tree absorbs ~22kg CO₂ per year.

Spread Awareness

Share eco-knowledge and inspire others. Your voice can bring more people into the green movement.
